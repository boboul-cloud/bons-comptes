Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a campaign?

Open the app, tap the "+" button in the top right corner, name your campaign, add participants, and you're all set.

How do I share a campaign with friends?

Open your campaign and tap "Share". You can send a web link via SMS, email, or any other method. Your friends don't need to install the app to view the expenses.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All your data is stored only on your device. No server, no account, no data collection. Sharing is done through links containing the encoded data, with no intermediary server.

What does Premium include?

Premium unlocks unlimited campaigns, PDF export, receipt scanner, SEPA QR code, proximity sharing, backups, and Live Activity. It's a one-time purchase with no subscription.

I purchased Premium but my features aren't unlocked

Go to Settings and tap "Restore purchases". If the issue persists, contact us by email with your Apple ID.

How do I delete my data?

Delete your campaigns in the app or uninstall the application. All data will be erased as nothing is stored on any server.

Does the app work offline?

Yes, completely. The app requires no internet connection. You can create campaigns, add expenses, and check balances without network access.

How does proximity sharing work?

Proximity sharing uses Bluetooth and Wi-Fi to send a campaign directly to nearby devices without internet. Both devices must have the app installed.

Useful Guides

  • Custom splitting: when adding an expense, choose "Custom amounts" or "Percentages" for a tailored split.
  • SEPA QR Code: on the balances screen, tap a suggested reimbursement to generate a banking QR code that the debtor can scan with their banking app.
  • Receipt scanner: tap the scanner icon in a campaign, take a photo of the receipt, and items will be detected automatically.
  • Backups: in Settings, create manual backups of your data and restore them if needed.

System Requirements

iOS 17.0 or later. Compatible with iPhone and iPad.
