🔒 Privacy Policy

Bons Comptes

Last updated: March 29, 2026

Introduction

Bons Comptes respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your personal data.

Data Collected

The app does not collect any personal data. All information (names, expenses, reimbursements) is stored only on your device.

Data Storage

Your data is saved locally on your device in JSON format. No data is sent to any external server. Data sharing is only done at your initiative via a link or JSON export.

Data Sharing

When you share a campaign via a web link, the data is encoded directly in the URL. No intermediate server stores this information.

Your Rights

You can delete all your data at any time by removing campaigns in the app or uninstalling the app. In accordance with GDPR, you have the right to access, rectify, and delete your data.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at bob.oulhen@gmail.com.
